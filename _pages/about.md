I am a fifth-year ME/CS undergraduate student at Georgia Tech.

My research interests lie in the intersection between robotics and machine learning. I am particularly interested in how robots can intelligently interpret their surrounding environment and perform complex decision making given sensor information of various modalities. I believe that the next step for robotics will be twofold: moving towards Spatial AI and extending the capabilities of SLAM, as well as interpretable deep learning methods for performing intelligent decisions. I also believe that there is a rich area of research for developing tools which combine traditional nonlinear optimization methods and probabilistic graphical models with modern data-driven deep learning.
